Our Inspiration

Receiving meaningful notes from your loved ones is one of the most heart-warming feelings in this world. With the recency of Valentines, we were even more appreciative of the letters written to us by someone special to us. However, we felt like there was no easy way for those with seeing disabilities to receive the same experience. This is why we came up with Braille-iant - an easy to use, one of a kind, braille printer that allows for card customization. With computer vision, and a heck of a lot of hardware, we are providing new ways for loved ones to show their true feelings for one another. Together, we can do something Braille-iant.
